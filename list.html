Bekti Bisaya ra Bidesh

In the face of challenging economic conditions and limited opportunities at home, an increasing number of young citizens are venturing abroad in search of work. This growing trend highlights the harsh reality of poor conditions and limited prospects in their home country. Faced with high unemployment rates, stagnant wages, and limited career growth, these young citizens are driven by the desire to secure a brighter future for themselves and their families. Their decision to explore work opportunities abroad reflects their determination to break free from the constraints imposed by their local circumstances, seeking greener pastures and a chance to thrive in more favorable environments. Despite the sacrifices and uncertainties they may encounter, their bold step signifies their resilience and aspiration for a better life, highlighting the pressing need for improved conditions and opportunities within their own communities.

Samaya Sandarva

Time is a precious resource that we all possess, and how we choose to allocate it reflects our priorities and responsibilities. Each individual has a unique set of duties that they must fulfill, ranging from personal commitments to professional obligations and social responsibilities. The way we manage our time is crucial in maintaining a balance between these various duties. It requires discipline, organization, and the ability to prioritize tasks effectively. By recognizing the importance of time and honoring our duties, we can make the most of each day, accomplishing what needs to be done while also nurturing our personal growth, relationships, and well-being. Time is a constant reminder that our actions and choices shape our lives, and by fulfilling our duties with diligence and purpose, we can create a meaningful and fulfilling existence.
